About Us

The Spirit of New Mexico, At Mach Speed

Founded by veteran pilot Scott R. Weaver, Team Zia Jet Racing carries the fearless spirit of New Mexico into the skies. Based in the Land of Enchantment, our team pushes the boundaries of speed, precision, and courage in every race.

From the sun-scorched mesas to the roar of jet engines on the tarmac, Team Zia represents the relentless pursuit of victory — one race at a time.

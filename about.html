ABOUT TEAM ZIA

Founder & Lead Pilot

Scott R. Weaver

Scott R. Weaver's love affair with the sky began long before he ever strapped into a cockpit. Growing up in New Mexico, surrounded by endless desert horizons and wide-open skies, he knew from an early age that he was meant to fly. By 2005, Scott had completed his first solo flight, and from that moment on, aviation became more than a passion — it became his life's purpose.

Following his calling to serve, Scott entered military service in 2010, where he honed his skills as a disciplined aviator under the most demanding conditions imaginable. Military aviation taught him the precision, situational awareness, and split-second decision-making that would later define his racing career. Those years forged a pilot who could remain calm under pressure and push both himself and his aircraft to the absolute limit.

"Speed isn't just about how fast you fly — it's about how precisely you cut through everything standing in your way."

— Scott R. Weaver

After returning to civilian aviation in 2018, Scott discovered the world of competitive jet racing — and it ignited something fierce within him. The combination of raw speed, tactical strategy, and sheer adrenaline was unlike anything he had experienced. He began dreaming of building a team that could compete at the highest levels while carrying the proud spirit of his home state with him.

In 2024, that dream became reality. Scott founded Team Zia Jet Racing, naming it after the sacred Zia sun symbol that represents the values of New Mexico: strength, unity, and an unwavering connection to the land. With a hand-picked crew, a race-ready fleet, and an unrelenting drive to win, Scott leads Team Zia into its inaugural 2026 race season with the same fearless determination that has defined his entire career.

What Drives Us

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To compete at the highest levels of professional jet racing while representing the bold, fearless spirit of New Mexico. Team Zia is committed to pushing the boundaries of speed and precision, inspiring the next generation of aviators, and proving that greatness can emerge from anywhere — even the sun-scorched deserts of the Land of Enchantment.

Our Vision

To become a dominant force in international jet racing, recognized not only for our speed on the course but for our integrity, innovation, and the deep-rooted values we carry into every competition. We envision a future where Team Zia stands on podiums around the world — a testament to what relentless passion and New Mexico grit can achieve.

Core Principles

Our Values

Speed

Our Journey

The Road to Race Day

2005

First Solo Flight

Scott R. Weaver takes to the skies alone for the first time over the New Mexico desert, igniting a lifelong passion for aviation that would eventually lead him to the world of jet racing.

2010

Military Service

Scott enters military aviation, where he develops the discipline, precision, and tactical instincts that would become the foundation of his racing career. Years of rigorous training forge an elite aviator.

2018

Return to Civilian Aviation

After completing his military service, Scott transitions back to civilian aviation and discovers the electrifying world of competitive jet racing — a perfect match for his skill set and competitive drive.

2022

The Zia Dream

Scott begins laying the groundwork for his own racing team, drawing inspiration from New Mexico's Zia sun symbol and its values of strength and unity. The vision for Team Zia Jet Racing takes shape.

2024

Team Zia Founded

Team Zia Jet Racing is officially established. Scott assembles a world-class crew, acquires the fleet, and begins intensive preparations for competitive racing at the highest level.

2026

First Race Season

Team Zia takes to the skies competitively for the first time, entering the Reno National Championship Air Races and marking the dawn of a new era in jet racing. The dream becomes reality.
